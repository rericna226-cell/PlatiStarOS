2.Personal data we collect

Through this website's contact form, we collect directly and by electronic means only the following personal data:

  • Full name.
  • Company or organization.
  • Email address.
  • Any information you freely choose to include in the message field, as well as the selected type of interest (NDA, licensing, acquisition, integration, or technical inquiry).

We do not collect sensitive personal data, nor financial or patrimonial data, through this website.

3.Purposes of processing

Primary purposes

Your data is used for the following purposes, which give rise to and are necessary for our relationship with you:

  • To attend to, follow up on, and respond to your request for information, NDA, licensing, acquisition, or integration.
  • To contact you by electronic or telephone means in order to coordinate technical conversations, due diligence, or intellectual property transfer processes.
  • To identify and qualify the applicant as a counterparty or potential partner.
  • To comply with obligations arising from the relationship that may eventually be established.

Secondary purposes

We do not currently use your personal data for secondary purposes such as institutional communications, advertising, or newsletters. If this changes in the future, you will be informed in due course and may object as described in section 7 of this notice.

4.Consent

By providing your personal data through this website's form and submitting your request, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Notice and grant your consent for the processing of your data in accordance with the purposes described herein. Consent may be revoked at any time as provided in section 6.

5.Transfers of personal data

The Controller will not transfer your personal data to third parties without your consent, except in the situations permitted by law, for example when a transfer is provided for by regulation, is necessary for the administration of justice, or occurs among companies within the same group under common policies.

If the Controller needs to transfer data to third parties other than the foregoing (currently, data is not shared with third parties), you will be informed and, when required by law, your consent will be obtained.

6.Means to exercise ARCO rights and revoke consent

You have the right to know what personal data we hold about you, what we use it for, and the conditions of such use (Access); to request correction when it is inaccurate or incomplete (Rectification); to request deletion from our records when you consider it is not being used in accordance with applicable rules (Cancellation); and to object to the use of your data for specific purposes (Opposition). These are known as ARCO rights.

To exercise any of these rights, or to revoke your consent, send a request to info@platistaros.com indicating:

  • Name of the data subject and the means by which you wish to receive the response.
  • Document proving your identity (or legal representation, if applicable).
  • Clear and precise description of the right you wish to exercise or of the specific request.
  • Any element that facilitates locating the personal data.

The Controller will respond to your request within the time periods established by applicable law.

7.Means to limit the use or disclosure of your data

You may request limitation of the use or disclosure of your personal data by sending your request to info@platistaros.com. Likewise, any communication you receive from us will include a mechanism that allows you to request that such communications cease.

8.Use of cookies and tracking technologies

This website does not use cookies or tracking technologies that automatically collect personal data.

9.Changes to this Privacy Notice

This Privacy Notice may be modified due to legal changes, internal practices, or new requirements. Any change will be published on this same page, indicating the date of the latest update. We recommend reviewing it periodically.

10.Authority regarding data protection

If you believe your right to the protection of personal data has been violated, you may file a complaint with the competent authority regarding personal data protection held by private parties in Mexico.

Questions about this notice?

For any inquiry related to the processing of your personal data, please write to info@platistaros.com.
